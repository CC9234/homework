Final Fantasy is a renowned role-playing game series developed by Square Enix (formerly Square).
Since its debut in 1987, the series has been celebrated for its rich storytelling, innovative battle systems, and iconic music.
Each installment typically features a standalone world and cast, blending fantasy and science fiction elements.
Spanning both single-player and online titles, Final Fantasy has become one of the most influential RPG franchises worldwide.
